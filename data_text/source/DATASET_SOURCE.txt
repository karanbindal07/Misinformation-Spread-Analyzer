Dataset name: ISOT Fake News Dataset
Authors: Ahmed, H., Traore, I., & Saad, S.
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/clmentbisaillon/fake-and-real-news-dataset
Downloaded on: 2026-01-19

Description:
The ISOT Fake News Dataset contains news articles labeled as fake or true.
Articles are collected from multiple online news sources and cover political
and general news topics.

The dataset is distributed as two CSV files:
- True.csv
- Fake.csv

Usage in Project:
This dataset is used as the text-only data source for Stage 2 of the project,
enabling content-based misinformation classification.
